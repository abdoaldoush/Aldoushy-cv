Dr. Aldoushy Mahdy

About Me

Dr. Aldoushy Mahdy, an Assistant Professor at Al-Azhar University, specializes in aquatic ecology with a focus on marine biology, conservation, and environmental research. With years of academic and field experience, Dr. Mahdy has made significant contributions to marine ecology, especially in the Red Sea region.
